# Simona AI

**Speech-to-text for Cypriot Greek.**

Most speech recognition tools are built for standard Modern Greek or English. **Simona AI** is aimed at people who speak the **Cypriot dialect** — podcasts, interviews, voice notes, field recordings, or personal projects where generic ASR often mishears words, drops dialect forms, or returns text that does not match how people actually talk in Cyprus.

Upload or record audio and get a transcription tuned for **Κυπριακά**, not “generic” Greek.

## Who is it for?

- Anyone working with **Cypriot Greek** audio who needs a quick transcript  
- Creators, researchers, and learners who want text from dialect speech without manual typing  

## Limitations

Works best with clear speech; not meant for critical decisions without a human checking the result.
